Wasfaty aims to provide and secure patient access to medicines, as well as to improve and develop drug use guidelines

An additional access channel is linking hospitals and primary health care centers to private pharmacies

Goals And Features

System Features
  • Ease of use
  • Automatic treatment of conflicting and inappropriate drugs
  • Easy access to a patient's medication history and history
  • Statistics and reports on the number of prescriptions and medicines disbursed
  • The patient has access to the instructions for using the medicine electronically
  • The possibility of returning medicines to patients with chronic diseases
  • The possibility of printing the details of the prescription by the doctor to the patient
  • Integration with HIS or work as an independent unit
Goals
  • Save time and resources
  • Facilitating disbursement of medication
  • Minimize errors that may occur in paper writing
  • Support, control, inventory planning and drug economics
  • Building a national database
  • The possibility of conducting studies, statistics and research that may serve all health sectors and related parties

Benefits

Suppliers
  • Dealing with pharmacies
  • Secure payment gateway
  • Spread of supplier drugs in pharmacies
  • Availability of medicines as an option available to all doctors working in the public health sectors
External Pharmacies
  • Payment facilities
  • Increase the number of customers, which is reflected positively increase the sales of pharmacy
  • Enhancing the role of the private sector in service delivery
  • Electronic connectivity to primary care centers, hospitals and suppliers
